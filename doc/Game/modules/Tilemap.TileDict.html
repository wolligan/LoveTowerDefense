Module Tilemap.TileDict

TileDict saves visual representation of tiles and mechanics of tiles.

Members of a tile:

name – string with the name of the tile.

draw – function that gets called for rendering the tile.

drawParams – parameters of the draw function.

color – color of the tile as {r,g,b}.

speed – multiplicator of the speed, 1 changes nothing.

isObstacle – a character cannot walk of an obstacle.

update – function that gets called before rendering

Info:

Tilemap.tileDict
Tile Dictionary for LightedTowerDefense

Fields:

  • air1 White standard tile being no obstacle and white and having no speed change
  • air2 Light grey standard tile being no obstacle and white and having no speed change
  • sand sand lets characters walk slowlier than on air tiles
  • obstacle characters cannot walk over this kind of tile
  • spawner dark mobs spawn here when a tick is sent to the update function
  • towerBuildPoint obstacle where a tower can be built
  • waypointForLighting waypoint for light mobs
  • waypointForDarkness waypoint for dark mobs
